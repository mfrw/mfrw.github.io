  • Question Similarity

    • We developed a model using deep learning and IR techniques to identify similarity of question. The dataset used for this project was the Quora-Question Similarity Dataset. We also trained the model on the MSRP, and the final accuarcy of our model was close to 80%. The current state of the art is at 85%. The approach we took is a vectorized the words, then fed it into a Bi-LSTM and trained the model on a GPU using tensorflow as backend.
    Advisor: Dr. Harshit & Dr. Sameep (IBM Research Labs)
